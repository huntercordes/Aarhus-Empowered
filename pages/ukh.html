Ungdomskulturhuset

A safe and secure space for young people to unfold, find peace of mind and be creative. You decide whether to come by for a cup of coffee, to create, or to contribute to what the house should incorporate. You understand UKH by experiencing it. We believe that the transition from ideas to action must be as short as possible. That is why all young people are encouraged to step inside and use the house.
